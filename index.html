About IMPETUS

IMPETUS is our Annual National-Level Technical Extravaganza. It is a two-day technical fest happening in UVCE since the year 2001 with a different theme every year, which emphasizes on the particular year's trending and concerning topics. IMPETUS serves as a platform for many participants to showcase their ideas and talents, as this fest is a hub of many Flagship events, General events and Gaming Arenas. With years passing, IMPETUS has witnessed gradual growth in participants count marking the highest of 2472 participants in the year 2023.


Flagship Events

IEEE UVCE hosts a various flagship events in IMPETUS every year, aimed at bringing together professionals, industries from different domains and backgrounds to network, learn, and share knowledge. Our flagship events are designed to provide attendees with valuable insights, practical skills, and industry updates. We offer a wide range of events to cater to different interests and needs, including conferences, workshops, webinars, and more.


General Events

IMPETUS is not just about our flagship events - we also welcome outside teams and individuals to organize events that align with our mission and values. These events are a great opportunity for the wider community to get involved with IMPETUS and showcase their skills and creativity. For IMPETUS 24.0, we have a wide range of exciting events planned that are sure to appeal to a diverse audience. Gear up and get involved - whether you're a developer, entrepreneur, tech enthusiastic, or just someone who loves to have fun, there's a place for you at IMPETUS 24.0!


Gaming Events and Arenas

Extending the boundaries, IEEE UVCE also hosts Gaming events and Arenas as a part of IMPETUS where the gaming enthusiasts get an opportunity to play along with participants from various colleges and universities. This includes games like BGMI, Valorant, Online Chess etc.

About Theme

IMPETUS 24.0 is hosted with a theme of “INDIA: THE FUTURE”. India, a land of diverse cultures, languages, and traditions, is on the cusp of a new era. The future of India lies in harnessing its potential as a knowledge economy, capitalizing on technological advancements, and nurturing its human capital. The developing India has brought with it an opportunity for all the budding talents to unleash their creativity for the welfare of the nation.


The theme hence encourages the participants to come up with innovative ideas and approaches for the developing country for whom the future belongs. With extending the boundaries and allowing the broad thinking among all the participants, the theme stands as a platform for all the innovators, creators, thinkers to expose their skills and talents. Marking from the developer who has brilliant ideas for our developing nation to the artist who has the foresight of a bright future for the country.

IEEE UVCE

IEEE UVCE is a Student Chapter of IEEE Bangalore Section functioning at UVCE. The branch was re-established in the year 2001 with the clear motto for Striding Ahead in terms of mastering technical skills and Knowledge. In the journey of more than 20 years, IEEE UVCE has conducted various events, workshops, webinars, mentorship programs, conferences, hackathons etc in order to help and encourage students in learning new skills and mastering their knowledge over the same. Along with this, IEEE UVCE has nested over 1000+ volunteers who have worked selflessly all these years in order to build a secure and steady future for themselves and their fellow mates. With all this, IEEE UVCE has also emphasized on serving humanity through many humanitarian activities such as school adoption, blood donation camp, clothes distribution camp etc. Continuing the same enthusiasm, IEEE UVCE strides to work for the betterment.
