Category: Flagship

Event Name: CodeStorm

Category: Flagship

Description: Participants will be asked to code a solution to the competitive programming problems, which requires the application of brainstorming logic and ideas. The event is divided into two rounds. In the first round the participants will be asked to solve 5 problems to test the knowledge and problem solving skills of the participants. The second round will require the participants to solve an optimization problem where the points will be scored based on the degree of optimisation.

Event Name: Mock Placements

Category: Flagship
